Jacob Brauer

VP Engineering · SymphonyRM · Irvine, CA · jbrauer@gmail.com

I build software used globally, currently in the Healthcare IT domain. I have led the development of open source, commercial, and cloud-based SaaS solutions.

As one of the initial developers of Mirth Connect, the open source integration engine, I played a major role in Mirth product development since 2006. Following Mirth's acquisition by NextGen Healthcare in 2013, I led the development of NextGen's Connected Health Solutions and launched Health Data Hub, a cloud-based data aggregation platform.

Currently, as VP Engineering at SymphonyRM, I am focused on building a platform that leverages data science in healthcare to drive value for patients, providers, and health systems.
